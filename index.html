My Timeline

Hangzhou, China

I was born and raised in a beautiful city in China.

D.C.,United States

I moved to the U.S when I was 18. I attended a quirky liberal art college called St. John's College, where I studied philosophy and history of math and science. In my spare time I love to make art and I was awarded the prize of best art for the senior class.

Aix-en-Provence, France

In my junior year of college, I studied abroad in France. I took painting and french classes while exploring the romantic Provence scenery .

Chicago,United States

I recently graduated from the University of Chicago with a MA in Art History. My research primarily focuses on 19th century French art, exhibition practices and the culture exchange between China, Japan and Europe.
